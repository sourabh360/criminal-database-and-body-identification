ABOUT US

This website will be used to help people and families to find their loved ones who are missing. The idea is to match the available images of missing people to the images in database. The image search will be happen irrespective of the time span that is, user will be able to compare the current images with the old images or vice versa as available in database. The image search will be powered by AI to identify the images irrespective of time.

Utilities

  1. To help tracing missing people
  2. For security reasons for criminal activities
  3. Unidentified bodies etc.
